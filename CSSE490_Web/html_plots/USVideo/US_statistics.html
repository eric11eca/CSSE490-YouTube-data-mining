Famous By Disliked

There is no such a thing as bad, a video can acutally gain polularity by being disliked. The chart on the right selected the top 10 videos with highest counts of dislikes. As the the chart shows, most of the disliked videos actually have a really high counts of likes also. In real life, different people have different ideas towards a topic, sometimes, a video can be both liked and disliked by a large number of people. Without an absolute standard, we can't accurately categorize the video as a liked video or as a disliked video.

Predict Rating(likes) by Decision Tree

This is a decision tree classifier to predict the rating (likes) of the videos according to the comment counts, views, and categories. The results showed that when comment counts are low, the higher the view the better the rating. When comment counts are high, the category "music" and "entertainment" win more likes. The results is also validated by a random forest classifier which do a ensemble learning on multiple decision trees for selecting the optimal decision tree. The result is the same as the original decision tree.

Likes by Channels

The horizontal bar chart displays the the most famous channels in U.S. and their numbers of likes received. We found that talk shows are averagely more popular. Similarly, TV shows our radio shows that are related to sports are welcomed by people. Besides, we also found that popularity of a channel is also related to its time of playing. Late night shows are liked by people more which is possibly because people have more free time at night.
